Best International Schools in Bangalore

Established on July 14th, 2003 with an initial enrollment of 135 students, Indus International School in Bangalore has since become one of the most esteemed international school in Bangalore. The school’s core values of love, respect, empathy, and discipline are the foundation of its commitment to creating a global ecosystem for students from an early age. Over the years, the school has grown exponentially, now boasting over 1100 students from 33 different countries.

At Indus International School, we believe in a holistic approach to education. Our programs extend beyond academics to help shape well-rounded individuals who are prepared to take on the world and realize their full potential. We are dedicated to providing our students with the tools they need to succeed in all aspects of life, not just in the classroom.

Our commitment to excellence is evident in everything we do, from our highly qualified faculty to our state-of-the-art facilities. We strive to create an environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and a love of learning.

Why Indus International School Bangalore

If you’ve made the decision to enroll yourchild in an international school, your next step is to find the best one available. Look no further than Indus International School in Bangalore, which is widely regarded as one of the top international schools in Bangalore.

Indus International School boasts state-of-the-art facilities, but that’s not all. Their leadership Innovation program is designed to help each child reach their full potential. Indus International School, Bangalore also offers a robust student entrepreneurship program, which equips children with the skills they need to pursue their business dreams. With guidance from industry experts and online internships, students are well on their way to becoming the next generation of life entrepreneurs.
